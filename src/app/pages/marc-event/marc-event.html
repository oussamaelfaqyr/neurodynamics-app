About the Hackathon

Unleashing Innovation for a Better Tomorrow

MARC Hackathon 2025 challenges participants to leverage AI and Robotics to create impactful solutions for individuals with disabilities.

The Challenge

This year, MARC Hackathon 2025 is all about "AI & Robots in Human's Service!" Participants will dive deep into creating innovative AI-powered robotic solutions. The focus is on improving the lives of individuals with disabilities by enhancing accessibility, mobility, and overall quality of life.

Whether it's developing smart prosthetics, intelligent navigation systems, or assistive robots for daily tasks, teams will have the opportunity to make a tangible difference.
